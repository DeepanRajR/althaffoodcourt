about us

We promise to put off your Hunger with our tasty delights



Very precisely, we started our brand "ALTHAF FOOD COURT" as small in the year 2016, with dreams of growing big, like other outlets. But our unique taste and quality offerings gave us a place in customers heart & today we are here right in front of you with presence in 4 places (Saidapet, Nanganallur, Selaiyur & Velachery) in Chennai city, with much more outlets in pipeline in major cities across Tamil Nadu.
Our key offerings are Arabian & Chinese cuisines.
Our signature menus are Shawarma & Grill Chicken.
In order to make our outlet a favourite for customers of all age groups, we extended our menu list with chicken starters, fried rice, fried noodles & few more to the list.
Through our tasty delights and constant focus on Quality and Quantity @ a much affordable price, made us stand-out against competition & not to forget
"OVERWHELMING RESPONSE & SELFLESS SUPPORT" from customers formed us a major reason what we are today
We welcome Franchise outlets & our franchise offering is widely embraced by many franchise holders. More tie-up outlets are in pipeline and will be live soon in many spots across Chennai. Those who find interest in Franchise option may please contact us on 9500 11 4763
Thanks for hearing to our story and sure will be a history
